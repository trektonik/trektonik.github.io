general faq’s

Where is my shipment?

The APTO Shipping & Transport Tracking page provides up-to-date information on the location of your shipment. Simply enter the 10 digit waybill number.

I want to send a shipment, but don't know what paperwork is required?

If you are sending a non-document shipment to a destination outside the EU, you will need to attach Customs paperwork. You can contact us and get your Customs paperwork in minutes.

What is the maximum size of package I can send?

Maximum weights and dimensions depend on the APTO Shipping & Transport service you choose.

What payment methods can I use with APTO Shipping & Transport?

A APTO Shipping & Transport Regional account is highly recommended if you ship frequently. This offers you low rates, monthly billing and extended access to APTO Shipping & Transport services.
